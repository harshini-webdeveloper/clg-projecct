Privacy Policy

Privacy policy is a legal document that describes how a website collects, uses, retains, and discloses the personal information of its users. It outlines what information is collected, how it is used, and the measures taken to protect the user's data. A website's privacy policy should be transparent, detailed, and accessible to users. It is important for websites to have a privacy policy to build trust with their visitors and to comply with legal requirements.
